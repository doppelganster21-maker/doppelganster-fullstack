What is a Doppelganger in D&D 5e?

In Dungeons & Dragons 5e, a doppelganger is a sentient shapeshifter capable of mimicking any humanoid it has seen. These monsters thrive on chaos, espionage, and identity theft within campaigns.

Doppelganger Stats (5e)

  • Armor Class: 14 (Natural Armor)
  • Hit Points: 52 (8d8+16)
  • Speed: 30 ft.
  • STR: 11 | DEX: 18 | CON: 13
  • INT: 13 | WIS: 14 | CHA: 16

Special Abilities

  • Shapechanger: Can transform into any humanoid it has observed.
  • Ambusher: Excellent at surprise attacks and infiltration.
  • Mind Reading: Gains insights into targets' behavior.

How to Use Doppelgangers in Campaigns

  • Create mystery-driven quests
  • Replace trusted NPCs for shock value
  • Introduce paranoia and tension among party members
  • Build political espionage storylines

Roleplay & Strategy Tips

  • Make them recurring villains
  • Give them secret motivations
  • Use them as spies or assassins
