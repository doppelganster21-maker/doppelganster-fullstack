Doppelganger 5e: Dungeons & Dragons Monster Guide

In Dungeons & Dragons 5e, a doppelganger is a shapeshifting monster capable of assuming the identity of any humanoid. This guide covers stats, abilities, and campaign tips for using doppelgangers in your games.

Doppelganger Stats (5e)

  • Armor Class: 14 (natural armor)
  • Hit Points: 52 (8d8+16)
  • Speed: 30 ft.
  • Abilities: Strength 11, Dexterity 18, Constitution 13, Intelligence 13, Wisdom 14, Charisma 16

Special Abilities

  • Shapechanger: Transform into any humanoid you’ve seen.
  • Ambusher: Can surprise and infiltrate groups.
  • Detection: Keen senses for deception detection.

Using Doppelgangers in Campaigns

  • Perfect for mystery and intrigue adventures.
  • Can create confusion in political plots or criminal encounters.
  • Introduce them as recurring NPCs for suspense.
