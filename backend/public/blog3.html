The Correct Term — What Is a Doppelganger?

The word doppelganger comes from German and means “double walker.” Today, it refers to:

  • a person who looks strikingly similar to you
  • a non-related look-alike
  • a “face twin” found by coincidence

Modern usage has nothing to do with folklore — most people use the word casually to describe someone who resembles them or celebrities.
